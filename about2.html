Hello, I’m Jorina!🫶🏼

I am a California-based Product Designer and UX Researcher passionate about understanding the user’s experience and creating inclusive designs that people will enjoy using!

Currently: I am an Informatics student at the University of California Irvine specializing in Human-Computer Interaction.

What’s Next: I want to enhance my understanding of designing for different problem spaces and learn to think critically about the products I create. Also, I want to continue designing interfaces and researching customer-facing solutions that humanize the user’s experience.
